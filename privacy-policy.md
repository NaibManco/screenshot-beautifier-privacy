# Privacy Policy - Screenshot Beautifier

**Effective Date:** 2026-03-26  
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-26

Screenshot Beautifier is designed to process screenshots entirely on your device.

## 1) Local-only processing
All screenshot capture, editing, and export operations are performed locally in your browser environment on your device.

- We do **not** upload your screenshots or edits to any remote server.
- We do **not** transmit image contents to third-party services.
- We do **not** run cloud-based image processing for extension functionality.

## 2) Data collection and uploads
Screenshot Beautifier does not collect personal data for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

- No screenshot content is uploaded by default.
- No account is required to use core extension features.
- No external API is required for screenshot beautification features.

## 3) Local storage usage (including temporary capture blobs)
The extension may store the following data locally on your device:

- User preferences (for example, styling and export settings) via browser extension local storage.
- **Temporary capture blobs in IndexedDB** during capture/edit workflows to support local processing and export reliability.

These temporary blobs are used only for local feature operation.

## 4) Retention and cleanup
Temporary capture blobs in IndexedDB are not intended for long-term retention.

- Temporary capture data is cleaned up on a best-effort basis during normal workflow transitions and when no longer needed for ongoing editing/export operations.
- If the browser or extension is interrupted unexpectedly (for example, crash/forced close), temporary data may persist until the next successful cleanup cycle or until you clear extension/site data.
- If you uninstall the extension or clear extension/site data, locally stored data (including IndexedDB data) may be removed by the browser.

## 5) Permissions context
The extension requests only permissions needed to provide screenshot functionality (for example, active tab capture, scripting support, local storage, downloads, and clipboard write where applicable).

We do not request broad host permissions for background tracking.

## 6) Third-party sharing
We do **not** sell or share screenshot contents with third parties.

## 7) Changes to this policy
If this Privacy Policy changes, the "Last Updated" date will be revised in this document.
